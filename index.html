  • The volcano is a stratovolcano.

  • The volcano is formed form layers of rock and ash.

  • The volcano is in the middle of the lower island.

  • The volcano can release toxic gas, that burns the respiratory system and kill local wildlife.

  • Pyroclastic flow can travel at speeds higher then 100 km/h that can destroy plant life.


  • Preparation:

  • If there is a chance of eruption, stay inside of your building or evacuate the island.

  • If the volcano is certain to erupt, close all windows and doors to limit the entry of
    volcanic ash. Place damp towels at thresholds.

  • Assemble an emergency supply kit, including flashlights, food and water.


  • Mitigation:

  • Your roof is designed to be able to hold ash and debris without collapsing.

  • Refer to the local warning system regularly, to understand the state of the volcano.

  • Avoid leaving opened or cracked containers outside, due to the risk of volcanic ash.
